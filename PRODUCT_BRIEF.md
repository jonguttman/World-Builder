# Planet Architect — Product Brief (v1.0)

## One-liner
Shape a planet's physics, climate, and chemistry — then watch life emerge and evolve on its own.

## Core Promise
Players never control organisms directly. They control environmental variables; life adapts autonomously via an evolution engine.

## Design Pillars
1) Emergence over scripting
2) Observation over micromanagement
3) Clear cause-and-effect
4) Discovery as progression (Codex)
5) Ethical premium monetization

## Target Audience
- Simulation fans, science-curious, systems thinkers
- Ages 16+

## Primary Mode
Curated Challenge Campaign (Levels). Each level is a scenario puzzle:
- Fixed starting parameters
- Limited intervention budget
- Objective + failure states
- Unique mechanic focus

## Monetization
Free-to-download. Training Levels 1–3 free. Packs:
- Pack 1 (Levels 4–10): $4.99
- Pack 2 (Levels 11–20): $8.99

## MVP Scope
- Levels 1–3 implemented end-to-end
- Codex v1
- StoreKit 2 purchase flow (packs visible, pack gating enforced)
- Deterministic simulation engine + time scaling
- Save/load

## Success Metrics (initial targets)
- D7 retention > 25%
- Avg session > 30 min
- Conversion to Pack 1 > 8%
- Replay rate > 3 planets

## Non-Goals for MVP
- Consciousness/culture layer
- Multiplayer
- Planet sharing library
- Scenario editor
