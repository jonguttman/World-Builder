## App Name

## Subtitle

## Promotional Text
Create a planet from scratch — then watch life emerge, adapt, and surprise you across millions of years.

## Description
Planet Architect is a speculative evolution simulation where you shape the laws of a world — not the creatures within it.

Set gravity, atmosphere, oceans, and climate. Then let evolution do what it does best: adapt.

### What you do
- Design planetary conditions: gravity, atmosphere, oceans, seasons
- Guide ecosystems indirectly through climate and chemistry tools
- Fast-forward through epochs and watch new body plans emerge
- Track discoveries in the Codex
